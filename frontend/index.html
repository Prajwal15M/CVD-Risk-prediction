About the Project

How It Works

01

Capture

A retinal fundus photograph is captured using a standard ophthalmoscope or fundus camera. The image reveals the blood vessel network within the eye.

02

Input Data

Patient biometric data including age, gender, blood pressure, cholesterol, glucose levels, and lifestyle factors are recorded alongside the retinal image.

03

AI Analysis

Our deep learning model — trained on validated CVD datasets — processes the retinal image and biometric features to compute a cardiovascular risk score.

04

Results

A comprehensive risk assessment report is generated with the CVD probability, contributing factors, and personalized medical recommendations.
